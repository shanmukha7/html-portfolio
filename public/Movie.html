Best of Pawan Kalyan Movies I have watched till date!

Top 3 movies.

1. Gabbar Singh 2012

Gabbar Singh is a 2012 Indian Telugu-language action comedy film directed by Harish Shankar. The film stars Pawan Kalyan in the titular role, along with Shruti Haasan and Abhimanyu Singh in supporting roles. The story revolves around a fearless and unconventional police officer, Gabbar Singh, who takes on a notorious criminal gang terrorizing a village. The film is known for its high-energy action sequences, catchy music composed by Devi Sri Prasad, and Pawan Kalyan's charismatic performance. Gabbar Singh was a commercial success and received positive reviews from both critics and audiences.

2. Attarintiki Daredi 2013

Attarintiki Daredi is a 2013 Indian Telugu-language family drama film directed by Trivikram Srinivas. The film stars Pawan Kalyan, Samantha Ruth Prabhu, and Pranitha Subhash in lead roles. The story follows the journey of a wealthy businessman who goes to great lengths to reunite his estranged family. The film is praised for its engaging storyline, emotional depth, and memorable dialogues. Attarintiki Daredi was a massive commercial success and became one of the highest-grossing Telugu films of its time.

3. Vakeel Saab 2021

Vakeel Saab is a 2021 Indian Telugu-language legal drama film directed by Venu Sriram. The film stars Pawan Kalyan in the lead role, along with Nivetha Thomas, Anjali, and Prakash Raj in supporting roles.
